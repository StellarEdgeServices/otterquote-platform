Frequently Asked Questions

What does it cost me?

Nothing. OtterQuote is 100% free for homeowners. Contractors pay a small platform fee when they win your job.

Do you handle my insurance claim?

No. We're not a public adjuster. You file your own claim with your insurance company. We help you find the best contractor after you have a claim.

What if I don't have my insurance estimate yet?

No problem. We have a guided "Help Me" tool that walks you through requesting your estimate from your adjuster.
