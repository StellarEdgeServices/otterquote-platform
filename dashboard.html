🔄 Switch Contractors?

You are choosing to switch to a different Otter Quotes contractor. Here is exactly what happens:

What changes immediately:
  • Your current contract is voided
  • Your project returns to open bidding for the entire contractor network
  • Your original contractor is notified and released from the project
  • Their platform fee is refunded in full — you are not charged

You will receive bids from other qualified contractors in your area. Once you select a new contractor, a new contract will be sent for signing.

⏱ This option is available up to 3 days before your scheduled installation date. After that cutoff, switching is no longer available.

You do not need to contact your current contractor directly — Otter Quotes handles the entire transition.
