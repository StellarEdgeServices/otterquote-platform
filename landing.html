The Problem: How Do You Know Who to Trust?

When you need a roofing or exterior project done, you're flooded with options. Door-knockers, cold calls, and pressure tactics from contractors you've never heard of. How do you know who to trust? How do you know if the price is fair?

Otter Quotes flips the script. Instead of contractors chasing you, they compete for your project — transparently. You see multiple bids from licensed professionals in your area, all competing to earn your business with better prices, warranties, and customer service.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Otter Quotes really free? ▼
Yes, completely. Otter Quotes is 100% free for homeowners. Contractors pay a platform fee when they win a project. We make money when we help contractors find customers — never by charging homeowners.
How is this different from Angi or HomeAdvisor? ▼
Those platforms sell your information to multiple contractors — you'll get flooded with calls and emails. Otter Quotes is different. We manage the entire process end-to-end: gathering contractor licensing and insurance information, helping you compile estimates, facilitating transparent bidding, and delivering a signed contract. Your information is only shared with contractors YOU select.
Do you handle my insurance claim? ▼
No, we're not a public adjuster. We don't negotiate with insurance companies. What we do is help you find the best contractor after you've filed your claim. We help you gather measurements and documentation so you can get the best bids. Some contractors can help with additional documentation, but the primary relationship is between you and your insurance company.
What areas do you serve? ▼
Otter Quotes is expanding across Indiana. Start your project at the link above — if contractors aren't yet in your area, we'll let you know and keep you on the list.
How fast do I get bids? ▼
Most homeowners receive their first bid within 24-48 hours. Once you've uploaded your project details (measurements, photos, insurance estimate if available), contractors can start bidding immediately. The speed depends on your location and the complexity of your project, but we've optimized our process to get bids into your hands as fast as possible.
What if I'm not happy with my contractor? ▼
You can switch to another Otter Quotes contractor at no cost up to 3 days before your scheduled installation start date. If something doesn't feel right before installation begins, switching is simple and free through your dashboard.
