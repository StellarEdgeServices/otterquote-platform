Common Questions

How fast will I get quotes?

How quickly you get your quotes depends on your location and the type of work needed. We work to get contractor quotes to your inbox as fast as possible — typically within a few business days of your project going live. We'll let you know as soon as they come in.

Can Otter Quotes help if I have an insurance claim?

Yes — but it's important to be clear about what we do and don't do. Otter Quotes does not file claims or negotiate with your insurance company. What we do is help homeowners who have already filed a claim get organized, get measurements, and get the best contractor for the repair. We make the contractor search easy — your insurer handles your claim.

Who sees my personal information?

Contractors see your project details and measurements — not your name, phone number, or address. We never sell your information to contractors, advertisers, or anyone else. The only people who see your contact information are the Otter Quotes team and the contractor you select.

What if I'm not happy with my contractor?

If something feels off before installation starts — the contractor went quiet, the timeline doesn't add up, you just changed your mind — you can switch to a different Otter Quotes contractor at no cost, up to 3 days before your scheduled start date. Otter Quotes handles the transition entirely. Your project goes back to the contractor network and a replacement is matched. You don't have to contact your current contractor at all.

Does Otter Quotes work for my type of project?

Yes. Otter Quotes works whether your insurance is covering the full cost of the repair, covering part of it, or you're paying entirely out of pocket. If you're not sure which situation applies to you, just describe what happened and we'll help you figure it out from there.

How do contractors pay? Is there a catch?

Otter Quotes is completely free for homeowners. Contractors pay a small percentage-based fee only when they earn your business. That's the model — no hidden fees, no subscriptions, no surprises for you.
