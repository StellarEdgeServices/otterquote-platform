Frequently Asked Questions

How does my policyholder get started?

+

They click your link, enter their info, and Otter Quotes helps them gather their insurance estimate, measurements, and material preferences. Contractors then compete for the job. The entire process is digital and takes about 10-15 minutes for the homeowner.

Does Otter Quotes represent my client in their claim?

+

No. Otter Quotes is NOT a public adjuster. We don't file claims, negotiate with carriers, or handle settlements. We help homeowners get the best contractor after they've filed their own claim. Your clients remain in control throughout the entire process.

Will this create any compliance issues?

+

Otter Quotes's referral program is designed for transparency. Commissions are paid for completed jobs, not for steering clients. However, we recommend reviewing the program terms with your compliance team.

What if my client doesn't have their insurance estimate yet?

+

No problem. Otter Quotes has a "Help Me" flow that assists homeowners in requesting their estimate from their adjuster. We guide them through the process so they can continue without waiting.

Do my clients pay anything?

+

Hover measurements are optional and paid upfront (rebated if they use an Otter Quotes contractor). The bidding and contract process is 100% free. Your clients pay nothing unless they choose to add measurements, which helps contractors provide more accurate bids.

What's the recruit bonus?

+

When you bring another agent to Otter Quotes using your recruit link, you earn $50 for every job over $10,000 that their referrals complete. It's one level only — you earn on the people you personally recruit, not on anyone they recruit. Your recruit link is on your partner dashboard.
