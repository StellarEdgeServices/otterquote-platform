Auto-Bid Settings
When auto-bid is enabled, OtterQuote will automatically place bids on your behalf for matching insurance full roof replacement opportunities in your service area.
Enable Auto-Bid for Insurance Roofing
OFF
How it works: When a homeowner in your service area submits an insurance claim for a full roof replacement, OtterQuote will automatically place a bid on your behalf at the insurance company's approved RCV (Replacement Cost Value) amount. The homeowner sees your bid instantly and can sign a contract right away.
Your Auto-Bid Will Match On:
✓ Funding type: Insurance claims only
✓ Scope: Full roof replacement only (not repairs)
✓ Trade: Roofing
✓ Price: RCV amount from the insurance estimate
✓ Location: Within your configured service area
Tip: Auto-bid gives you a huge advantage. Homeowners are far more likely to sign when a contract is ready immediately. You can still manually bid on opportunities that don't match your auto-bid criteria.

Repair Work

Repair jobs are smaller in scope than full replacements. They require shingle matching and on-site diagnostics. Opt in only if your crew is comfortable with repairs.

Accept Repair Opportunities
Appear in repair results and receive notifications for repair jobs in your service area

Show-Up Guarantee Agreement

By accepting repair work, you agree to the OtterQuote show-up guarantee: if you schedule a repair diagnostic visit and fail to appear without 24 hours notice, OtterQuote will pay the homeowner $100 and charge your account $250.
