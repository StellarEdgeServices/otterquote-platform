Hover 3D Measurement Report

Hover uses advanced technology to create a 3D model of your property from photos you take with your phone. The result is a professional measurement report accurate enough for contractors to provide detailed, precise bids.

How the $79 works: You pay $79 now to order your Hover measurement report. When your project closes with an Otter Quotes contractor, the full $79 is rebated to your original payment method. If you don't complete your project with an Otter Quotes contractor, you keep the Hover report — it's yours forever, reusable for future exterior projects.

Take Photos of Your Home

We'll send you a link to Hover's guided photo capture. Their app walks you through taking 8-10 photos from around your home — it checks photo quality as you go.

Receive Your Report

Hover processes your photos (usually 24-48 hours) and creates a detailed measurement report. We'll save it in your account — you'll get a notification when it's ready.

What you'll need: A smartphone with a decent camera and the ability to walk around the outside of your home. Hover's guided process handles the rest.
