How Money Flows Through Otter Quotes

End-to-end payment lifecycle from homeowner selection to contractor payout

1

Homeowner Selects Contractor

Homeowner reviews bids, compares contractors, and clicks "Select This Contractor." This triggers the contract signing flow.

2

Contract Signed via DocuSign

Contractor's own agreement is auto-populated with job details and e-signed by the homeowner. Product type is locked in (no color yet).

3

Platform Fee Charged to Contractor

Otter Quotes charges the contractor's card on file for the platform fee. This is the revenue event.

Platform fee = 5% of job value
4

Deductible Collected from Homeowner

Homeowner pays their insurance deductible via Stripe. Funds land in Otter Quotes's platform account — NOT the contractor's.

$2,500 → Otter Quotes Platform Balance
5

Funds Held in Platform Balance

Deductible sits in Otter Quotes's Stripe balance while work is performed. Homeowner protected — contractor doesn't have the money yet.

HELD — awaiting ACV check
6

ACV Check Verified

Insurance company issues the ACV (Actual Cash Value) check. Otter Quotes confirms payment was received by homeowner or contractor.

7

Deductible Released to Contractor

Otter Quotes creates a Stripe Transfer from platform balance to the contractor's Express Connected Account. Contractor receives funds in their bank account.

$2,500 → Contractor Bank Account

Legal flag (D-029): The deductible escrow structure requires attorney sign-off before go-live. Key question: does holding homeowner funds require an Indiana money transmitter or escrow license? This is tabled until legal review is complete. See attorney review brief in Otter Quotes/Legal/.

Stripe Connect Architecture

How the pieces fit together when we go live

Platform Account

Stellar Edge Services LLC

• Collects homeowner deductible payments

• Charges contractor platform fees

• Holds funds until release conditions met

• Creates Transfers to connected accounts

Connected Accounts (Express)

Each contractor gets one

• Stripe-hosted onboarding (KYC handled)

• Manual payouts (Otter Quotes controls timing)

• Express Dashboard for payout history

• Receives Transfers after ACV verification

Charge Type

Separate Charges & Transfers

• Charge lands on platform account

• Transfer created independently (delayed)

• Multiple transfers per charge supported

• Full refund control on platform side

Why Not Destination Charges?

• Destination charges move funds immediately

• No built-in "hold" mechanism

• Refunds require clawback from contractor

• Less control over escrow timing

Two Separate Payment Events

Event 1: Platform Fee

Who pays: Contractor

When: At contract signing

Amount: Tiered % of job value

Stays: In Otter Quotes balance (revenue)

Event 2: Deductible Escrow

Who pays: Homeowner

When: After contract signing

Amount: Insurance deductible

Released: To contractor after ACV check

Stripe hold limit: Funds can sit in your platform balance for up to 2 years (US). More than enough time — ACV checks typically resolve in weeks.
