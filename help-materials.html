What type of roofing material interests you?

Choose between shingles (most common) or metal roofing.

Shingles

The most popular choice. Easier installation, good durability, and widely supported by insurance policies.

Metal Roofing

Premium durability with 40+ year lifespan. Higher upfront cost but long-term value. Multiple styles available.

Which shingle type works for you?

Select your preferred style and durability level.

Architectural Shingle

Most Popular

The most popular choice. Dimensional look, 130+ mph wind rating. Included in most insurance claims at no additional cost.

Designer Shingle

Premium Upgrade

Premium appearance mimicking slate, cedar, or tile. Expect to pay the difference out of pocket.

3-Tab Shingle

Basic

Basic, flat appearance. Rarely used on new installations.
