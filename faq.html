The Process

For each document we need, you'll see two buttons: "Upload" (if you already have it) or "Help Me" (if you don't). The Help Me tools walk you through getting what you need. For example, if you don't have your insurance estimate yet, we can send a professional email to your adjuster requesting it — and automatically capture their response when they reply.

Hover creates a professional 3D model of your property from smartphone photos. This gives contractors accurate measurements for their bids. You take a few photos following Hover's guided instructions, and the report comes back within 24-48 hours. It's optional but gives you more accurate bids. If you use a OtterQuote contractor, we rebate the measurement fee. Either way, you keep the report forever for future projects.

Up to 5-6 contractors will see your claim and have the opportunity to bid. Not every contractor bids on every job, but competition is the whole point — more bids means a better deal for you.

Most bids come in within 24-48 hours. The bidding window stays open until you select a contractor — there's no deadline. Take your time and compare. We'll text you every time a new bid arrives.

After You Choose

You sign the contractor's agreement electronically (e-sign). Your contractor is required to contact you within 48 hours. After signing, you'll pick your shingle or metal color — we'll show you the manufacturer's color options and you can even see them on your actual house using their visualization tools. Or you can request a contractor visit with physical color boards.

Trust & Safety

Yes. Every contractor in our network provides proof of licensing, workers' compensation insurance, and general liability insurance. We display verification badges so you can see their credentials before you choose. You'll also see their years in business, customer reviews, and ratings.

Your data is stored securely with enterprise-grade encryption. Contractors only see the information they need to bid on your job — they don't get your full claim file or property report. We never sell your personal information.
