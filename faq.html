That's how we make money — by making contractors earn your business.

The Process

For each document we need, you'll see two buttons: "Upload" (if you already have it) or "Help Me" (if you don't). The Help Me tools walk you through getting what you need. For example, if you're working with insurance and don't have your insurance estimate yet, we can send a professional email to your adjuster requesting it — and automatically capture their response when they reply.

A professional measurement report uses aerial imaging technology to create an accurate model of your property — giving contractors precise measurements for their quotes. You take a few photos from your smartphone and the report comes back within 24-48 hours. It's optional but gives you more accurate quotes. If you use an Otter Quotes contractor, we rebate the measurement fee. Either way, you keep the report forever for future projects.

For Insurance Projects

Yes. If you've filed an insurance claim and received an adjuster's estimate, Otter Quotes helps you find the best contractor to handle the work. We manage the contractor bidding and contract process — your contractor coordinates the claim details directly with your insurance company. We never collect insurance proceeds.

Or you can request a contractor visit with physical color boards.

Trust & Safety

Otter Quotes collects and displays state licensing, workers' compensation insurance, general liability insurance certificates, public customer reviews (from Google, BBB, Angi, and Yelp), and years in business for every contractor on the platform. Otter Quotes does not perform criminal background checks. Our role is to gather the information you need to do your own due diligence — you choose the contractor that's right for your project.

Your data is stored securely with enterprise-grade encryption. Your information is only shared with contractors you approve — and only what they need to prepare a quote for your specific project. We never sell your personal information.
