**Multi-Trade Bidding — How it works on this opportunity**

This opportunity has two active trades. Contractors see three bid options:

1. **Roofing only** — bid just the roof, gutters remain open to other contractors
2. **Gutters only** — bid just the gutters, roof remains open
3. **Roofing + Gutters (bundled)** — bid both trades; enter a single total price with an optional bundled discount line item ("Bundle savings: −$X")

The homeowner can compare a contractor who bid both trades against separate specialists on each trade. The bid comparison view shows bundled bids with a "One contractor, one schedule" badge.

*This is the key interaction the static demo should show — the multi-trade bid form with the trade-selector step (Step 1 of the 3-step wizard per D-162) and the bundled pricing option.*
