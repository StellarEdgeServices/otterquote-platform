Everything you need to know about partnering with OtterQuote.

Getting Started

You need: valid business insurance (general liability + workers' comp), any applicable municipal licenses (we support multi-municipality licensing), and a completed company profile. We verify everything before activating your account.

Most applications are reviewed within 48 hours. Once approved, you can start browsing opportunities immediately.

Bidding & Pricing

When an opportunity matches your service area and preferences, you submit a bid including your price, warranty terms, estimated start date, and decking contingency. Homeowners see all bids side-by-side and choose the contractor they trust most.

A percentage of the contract value, charged only when you complete the project. The exact fee is displayed when you submit your bid so there are no surprises. No hidden fees, no monthly charges.

On every roofing bid, you provide a per-sheet price for replacing damaged decking and a full re-deck price. This is required because damaged decking is only discoverable after tear-off and is required by building code.

On RCV (replacement cost) jobs, homeowners are largely insulated from price — their insurance covers the full replacement cost minus deductible. You compete on materials, warranties, and reputation, not just price. On ACV (actual cash value) jobs, the homeowner pays the difference between the insurance payout and your bid out-of-pocket, so price matters more.

After Winning
