CONTRACTOR AGREEMENT

This Agreement is entered into as of the date of signing by and between the Homeowner (you) and the Contractor (as listed above).

1. SCOPE OF WORK: The Contractor agrees to provide the services outlined in the bid for the job scope listed above.

2. CONTRACT PRICE: The total price for the work is as stated in the bid. This price includes all labor, materials, and equipment necessary to complete the job.

3. PAYMENT TERMS: The deductible will be collected as a refundable deposit. Upon completion, the Contractor will invoice your insurance company. Once the insurance claim is processed and funds are received, the deductible will be held in escrow and released to the Contractor after insurance payment.

4. WARRANTY: All work is warranted as per the warranty terms listed in the key terms section above.

5. TIMELINE: Work is expected to begin on or around the estimated start date. The Contractor will contact you within 48 hours of contract signature to confirm the schedule and introduce themselves.

6. CHANGES: Any changes to the scope of work must be agreed upon in writing by both parties.

7. INSURANCE & LIABILITY: The Contractor maintains appropriate insurance coverage for all work performed.

8. DISPUTE RESOLUTION: Any disputes will be resolved according to ClaimShield's terms of service and applicable law.

By checking the box above and clicking "I Agree & Sign," you acknowledge that you have read this agreement, understand its terms, and agree to be bound by it.
