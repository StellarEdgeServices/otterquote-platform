How Otter Quotes Works

We walk you through every step — from describing your project to signing a contract with a contractor you trust.

Step 1

Create Your Account

Enter your name, email, phone, and address. We send you a secure magic link — no password needed. Your referring insurance agent or realtor may have already set this up for you.

Step 2

Build Your Project Package

Your dashboard shows everything contractors need to bid: project scope, property measurements, and material preferences. For each item, you can upload it yourself or click "Help Me" and we'll walk you through getting it. If you're working with insurance, we can even email your adjuster for you and automatically capture their response.

Step 2B — Optional

Get Professional Measurements

Want the most accurate bids? Get a professional aerial measurement report of your property. If you end up using an Otter Quotes contractor, we rebate the measurement fee. Either way, you keep the report forever for future projects.

Step 3

We Match You With Contractors

We match your project to licensed, insured contractors in your area. Contractors compete with their best bid. Watch bids arrive in real time on your dashboard — we'll text you each time a new one comes in.

Step 4

Compare & Choose

See all bids side by side — pricing, warranty terms, estimated start date, contractor reviews, and proof of insurance on file. Dive deep into any contractor's profile to learn more. Take your time — the bidding window stays open until you're ready to decide.

Step 5

Sign Your Contract

Once you choose a contractor, sign the contract electronically. Your contractor must reach out to you within 48 hours. After signing, you'll pick your shingle or metal color — using the manufacturer's visualizer tool to see it on your actual house.

Step 6

We Follow Up

After your contractor starts, we check in at every milestone — scheduling, installation day, and completion. If anything goes wrong, one tap brings us back in to help. We only ask for a review if you're happy with the work.

What Does This Cost?

Otter Quotes is 100% free for homeowners. Contractors pay a 5% platform fee on the contract total — that fee is already included in the price they quoted you, so you don't pay it separately. You pay nothing — not now, not ever.
