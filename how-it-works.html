How OtterQuote Works

We walk you through every step — from filing your claim to signing a contract with a contractor you trust.

📝
Step 1

Create Your Account

Enter your name, email, phone, and address. We send you a secure magic link — no password needed. Your referring insurance agent or realtor may have already set this up for you.

📄
Step 2

Build Your Claim Package

Your dashboard shows everything we need before contractors can bid: your insurance estimate, property measurements, and material preferences. For each item, you can upload it yourself or click "Help Me" and we'll walk you through getting it. We can even email your adjuster for you and automatically capture their response.

📐
Step 2B — Optional

Get Professional Measurements

Want the most accurate bids? Purchase a Hover 3D measurement report of your property. If you end up using a OtterQuote contractor, we rebate the measurement fee. Either way, you keep the report forever for future projects.

🎯
Step 3

We Match You With Contractors

We match your claim to licensed, insured contractors in your area. Contractors compete with their best bid for a fully executed contract. Watch bids arrive in real time on your dashboard — we'll text you each time a new one comes in.

⚖️
Step 4

Compare & Choose

See all bids side by side — pricing, warranty terms, estimated start date, contractor reviews, and insurance verification. Dive deep into any contractor's profile to learn more. Take your time — the bidding window stays open until you're ready to decide.

✍️
Step 5

Sign Your Contract

Once you choose a contractor, sign the contract electronically. Your contractor must reach out to you within 48 hours. After signing, you'll pick your shingle or metal color — using the manufacturer's visualizer tool to see it on your actual house.

✅
Step 6

We Follow Up

After your contractor starts, we check in at every milestone — scheduling, installation day, and completion. If anything goes wrong, one tap brings us back in to help. We only ask for a review if you're happy with the work.

What Does This Cost?

OtterQuote is 100% free for homeowners. Contractors pay a small platform fee on fully executed contracts. You pay nothing — not now, not ever.
