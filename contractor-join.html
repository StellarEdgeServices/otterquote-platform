Homeowner Cancellation Policy

Before you sign up, we want to be straight with you about one thing.

Homeowners on OtterQuote can switch to a different contractor in our network up to 3 days before their scheduled installation date. No questions asked. Sometimes we let the wrong homeowner through. Sometimes the homeowner changes their mind for no good reason at all. Whatever the case, it's going to happen occasionally — and we'd rather you know that upfront than be surprised by it.

Here's what that means for you:

  • OtterQuote handles the switch entirely. You don't make any calls, send any emails, or do anything.
  • Your platform fee is refunded in full. You're not out a dollar.
  • The homeowner moves to another contractor in the network under the same contract terms.

You'll lose a project here and there. You'll also pick up projects from homeowners who switched away from someone else. That's the deal. A platform where homeowners feel protected is a platform that keeps growing — and more homeowners on OtterQuote means more projects for every contractor in the network.
