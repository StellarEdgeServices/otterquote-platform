How Otter Quotes Works for Contractors

No cold calls. No door knocking. Just signed contracts delivered to your inbox — you pay only when you win.

⚡
Step 1

Apply in 60 Seconds

Your email address is all you need to get started. We send you a magic link — click it and you're in. No lengthy application, no waiting room, no callbacks.

🏗️
Step 2

Build Your Profile

During your application, you'll upload three items: your CGL Certificate of Insurance (naming Stellar Edge Services LLC as additional insured), your workers' compensation policy or Indiana WCE-1 Clearance Certificate, and your contractor license or no-license attestation. Once your documents are reviewed and approved, you're ready to receive signed contracts.

✅
Step 3

Get Approved

We review your profile to confirm licensing and insurance are in order. We make your research easy by gathering licensing, insurance, and review information all in one place for homeowners. Most approvals happen within 1–2 business days. Once approved, you start receiving opportunities matched to your service area and trades.

🎯
Step 4

Browse & Bid

Browse opportunities matched to your service area and the trades you handle. Each opportunity shows the project details, Hover aerial measurements, and the homeowner's insurance estimate — when available — so you know exactly what you're bidding on before you commit. Submit a competitive bid with full pricing transparency — the bid form shows your exact platform fee in real time. For insurance RCV projects, auto-bid submits your bid automatically at the RCV amount with your standard terms.

Bid validity: Each bid is active for 14 days. Enable auto-renew (per bid or as your default in Settings) and your bid stays live for up to 3 additional windows — 42 days total — without any manual effort.

🏆
Step 5

Win the Contract

When a homeowner selects you, the contract is sent via DocuSign for sequential signing — you sign first, then the homeowner signs second. Both signatures happen right here on our platform. Once both parties have signed, you receive the completed contract, the loss sheet, trade-specific Hover aerial measurements, and the homeowner's color and material selections — which are made before bids go out so you know exactly what you're building. The homeowner's contact information is released the moment the contract is fully signed.

📈
Step 6

Complete & Grow

Deliver great work. Build your reputation on the platform. Every completed project strengthens your profile and helps you stand out to homeowners comparing contractors. The more projects you complete, the more your track record speaks for you.

📐

Aerial Measurements

Trade-specific aerial measurements — accurate square footage, pitch, and facet data for your trade. Included when available.

Material & Color Selections

Name, phone, and email — released the moment the contract is signed. You have 48 hours to make initial contact.
