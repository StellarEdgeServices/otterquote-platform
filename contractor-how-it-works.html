How ClaimShield Works for Contractors

We're your sales team — without the truck, the manager, or the advance. Here's how it works.

📋
Step 1

Apply to Join Our Network

Submit your company info, insurance certificates, and any applicable municipal licenses. We verify everything and set up your profile. No upfront costs.

🎯
Step 2

Browse Available Opportunities

When a homeowner submits a claim, it appears in your opportunity feed. See the job type (insurance RCV, ACV, retail, or repair), location, estimated scope, and urgency level — all before you commit to bidding.

💰
Step 3

Submit Your Best Bid

Enter your pricing, warranty terms, estimated start date, and decking contingency. For insurance RCV jobs, your bid emphasizes materials and warranties over price. Your platform fee is calculated automatically.

🏆
Step 4

Win the Contract

The homeowner reviews all bids side-by-side and selects a contractor. When they choose you, the contract is executed electronically via DocuSign — using YOUR agreement, auto-populated with the project details.

📦
Step 5

Receive a Contract-Ready Package

You get: a fully executed contract, the insurance loss sheet, trade-specific Hover measurements, material selections, and homeowner contact info. Show up ready to work — no sales visit needed.

✅
Step 6

Complete the Job & Get Paid

Install the roof, we follow up with the homeowner at every milestone. Your platform fee is collected only after the job is complete. Happy homeowners leave reviews that build your ClaimShield reputation.

What Does This Cost?

You pay nothing upfront. ClaimShield charges a percentage-based platform fee only when you win and complete a project. No subscription fees, no pay-per-click, no wasted ad spend.
